Rescue is a HTML template for animal shelters. Easily display all of the pets available
at your shelter.

chance the banner_widget_count2 class to accomidate the number of info blocks you
have here, from 1 to 5, or more than that can be banner_widget_overflow and the
blocks will resize accordingly.
